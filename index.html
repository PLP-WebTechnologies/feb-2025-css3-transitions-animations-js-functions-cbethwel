Femur

The femur is the only bone located within the human thigh. It is both the longest and the strongest bone in the human body, extending from the hip to the knee.

Did You Know? / Quick Tip

Mnemonic Magic: Creating mnemonics (memory aids) can significantly improve your recall of complex anatomical terms and sequences. For example, to remember the carpal bones: "Some Lovers Try Positions That They Can't Handle."
